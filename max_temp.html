Max Temperature

In the above scatter plot we are showing the correlation between each indvidual city's latitude and max temperature. As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). The farther away the city is from this range temperature decreases. Temperature and latitude are inversely related. The closer you get to a latitude of 0 the higher the temperature. The farther away you are from a latitude of 0 the lower the temperture. Interestingly, during the time this data was collected, the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer. This may be due to the tilt of the earth during this time of year.
